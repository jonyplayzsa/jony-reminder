Why sound may not work

Apple requires website audio to be unlocked by a tap. That is why V5 has Start Sound / Alarm Engine.
  • Turn iPhone silent switch OFF.
  • Turn volume UP.
  • Keep the app open.
  • Press Start Sound / Alarm Engine before waiting for an alarm.
  • Press Test Sound Now. If that makes no sound, the phone/browser is blocking audio.
