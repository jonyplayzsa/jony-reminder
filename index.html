Why it may not ring

This is still a website app. iPhone can block sound if the app is closed, locked, in another tab, silent mode is on, or sound was not unlocked by a button tap.
  1. Open the app fresh.
  2. Tap Start Engine.
  3. Tap Test Sound Now.
  4. Tap 10-Second Alarm Test and keep the screen open.
